About LaunchMyCareer

LaunchMyCareer, a project of College Measures at American Institutes for Research, is designed to help

students, advisors, and parents learn about hot jobs, hot skills, and high Return on Investment (ROI)

programs in their state, so that they can find their best course. Examples include LaunchMyCareerTN ,

as well as similar sites in Colorado, Texas, and Florida.

This site includes resources for partners including guides for advisors working with both K-12 students

and adults returning to school, as well as materials focused on traditionally-aged high school students themselves.
